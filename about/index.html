FENWAY Center

We help brands stand out through powerful, elegant visual design. Our design philosophy is shaped by our experience growing up at our father’s bookbindery. Surrounded by the highest-quality materials and turn-of-the-century machines, we observed the art of true craftsmanship firsthand.

Today, that bookbindery is our brand design studio and we apply those same principles to our brand design work. We have joined forces with Workshop Built, a creative growth agency based in California, USA. Fusing American entrepreneurial ingenuity and European design excellence we have created a unified team that delivers solutions to brands looking to excel in today’s digital economy.

       
play Reel

Our composition creates a truly unique offering for a boutique studio. Our promise is to bring a dynamic, thoughtful perspective shaped by a commitment to big thinking and creative ideas that solve important challenges for our clients.

We are experts in Brand Identity, Brand Strategy, Brand Guardianship, Creative Direction, Naming, User Interface Design (UI/UX), Front-end Web Development, Motion Graphics, 3D Graphics, Animation, Stationery, Packaging, Production Management, Photography, Video, Illustration, Signage and Wayfinding, Creative Copywriting.
